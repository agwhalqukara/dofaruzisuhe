Nina Ansaroff MMA Stats And Net Worth

Nina Ansaroff is a mixed martial artist with a substantial win ratio on her account. She played sixteen games, where she registered victory in ten games and lost six matches. She won four matches by knockout, two by submission, and four by decisions.

Her last match was against Tatiana Suarez, which she lost by a unanimous decision. Similarly, her upcoming fight is against Mackenzie Dern on 10 April 2021. The match will proceed at UFC Apex in Las Vegas, Nevada, the United States.

Through her professional career as an MMA Fighter, she has earned a colossal net worth, which seems to be overshadowed. However, as per LiveSportWorld, Nina Ansaroff has an estimated net worth of $500 thousand.

Who Is Nina Ansaroff Dating?

Nina Ansaroff leads a same-sex relationship with a fellow mixed martial artist, Amanda Nunes. The lovebirds are engaged for a couple of years and have a long-term relationship for a few years.

Nina and Amanda got engaged back in 2018 and shared the happy news via Nunes' Instagram captioning 'shesaidyesss' and showcased their diamond ring.

Nearly after a couple of years, the pair announced that they were expecting their first child in March 2020. They shared a picture of themselves holding a cloth for their upcoming baby on Instagram.

They captioned, 'I am excited to announce that Baby Raegan Ann Nunes will be here mid-September! I cannot wait to meet her! I am carrying and still plan on fighter after she is born.' Also, they revealed that their due date was in September 2020.

As expected, Nina Ansaroff and Amanda Nunes welcomed their first child named Raegan Ann Nunes on 24 September 2020.
