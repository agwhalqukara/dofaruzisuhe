Barry Greenstein Biography

Barry Greenstein is one of the most popular and richest Poker Player who was born on December 30, 1954 in Chicago, Illinois, United States. Barry Greenstein (born December 30 1954, in Chicago, Illinois) is an American professional poker player. He has been a winner of a variety of major tournaments which include three wins in the World Series of Poker and two on the World Poker Tour. Greenstein makes a donation of his tournament wins to charitable causes, including Children, Incorporated, earning him the title “the Robin Hood of poker”. Greenstein was elected to the Poker Hall of Fame in the year 2011.

Greenstein has been a part of each of the three seasons in the series Poker Superstars Invitational Tournament and all seven seasons of the GSN series High Stakes Poker. He also appeared on the initial three seasons of the late-night series Poker After Dark on NBC. Greenstein has also taken home the two World Poker Tour (WPT) titles including the Fifth Annual Jack Binion World Poker Open ($1,278,370) as well as the in 2006, the WPT Invitational in the Commerce Casino ($100,000). Greenstein has also won two additional WPT titles at special events such as the WPT Father and Sons Tournament in 2006; WPT World Poker Finals; and WPT Poker by the Book Chapter 2. As of 2008, Greenstein started donating his net earnings to charities. In 2008, at the World Series of Poker (WSOP), Greenstein cashed in six events, resulting in an amount of $768,461. He was also third in the WSOP player of the year list which was in second place behind Erick Lindgren. As of 2011, Greenstein earned five wins during the World Series of Poker, including 3rd place for the $10,000 Limit Hold’em Championship for $169,512 and a 15th-place finish for the $50,000 Player’s Championship.

The year 2005 was the time Greenstein released a novel entitled Ace on the River. Tuan Le who is a WPT Season 3 champion, says that the book taught him how to be successful in the poker arena. Greenstein is well-known for giving away a edition of his work to any player who loses to him during a tournament, which includes his signature and the specifics of the winning hand. Greenstein has taught his ex-girlfriend Mimi Tran how to play poker in exchange for teaching him to talk Vietnamese. Greenstein also played on the internet at PokerStars under the alias “barryg1” and was a part of poker’s card room’s Team PokerStars.
