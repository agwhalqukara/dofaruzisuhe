Career Kickoff

In 2012, she was cast as Nona Clark in ABC's show 666 Park Avenue for six episodes. This role was an excellent platform to take-off. The young actress played Heather in 2014's "Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day" alongside Steve Carell, Jennifer Garner, and Ed Oxenbould. The movie made $101.4 million worldwide with a budget of $28 million. She also starred in multiple TV shows like Teen Wolf, NCIS, The Fosters, and a recurring role in widely popular Netflix show 13 Reasons Why until 2018.
